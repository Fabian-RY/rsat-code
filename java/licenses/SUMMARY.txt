SUMMARY LICENSES

NeAT_javatools.jar and the war files merge a number of jar files,
which are licensed as listed below.

The jar files are needed for data storage and access (postgresql, hibernate and its dependent jars),
web services (axis and its dependent jars) as well as graph conversion (gxl, jung) and
general utilities (commons-logging, commons-collections).
