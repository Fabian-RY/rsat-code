NeA-tools - Credits

The Network Analysis Tools integrate a set of programs implemented at the Laboratoire de Bioinformatique des Génomes et des R&ecaute;seaux (BiGRe), plus a few programs developed by third parties (with the authorization of their authors).

The authors are indicated in the manual pages of each tool.

  • The programs for handling graphs and clusters (conversion, comparisons, display) were mainly developed by Sylvain Brohée and Jacques van Helden.

  • The algorithms for path finding in metabolic networks were principally developed by Fabian Couche, Didier Croes, and Karoline Faust, under the supervision of Jacques van Helden.
